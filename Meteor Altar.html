The Meteor Altar is a pre-Hardmode furniture item that can be crafted or found naturally in the meteorite biome afte defeating Skeletron. Right-clicking on the altar at night will spawn (Unnamed Boss 1) if the player has at least one hm in their inventory. Once the Meteor Altar is used the screen will shake violently for a few moments, and (Unnamed Boss 1) will spawn in. Attempting to use the altar during the day does nothing.
